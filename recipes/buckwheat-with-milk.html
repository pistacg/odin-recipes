Buckwheat with milk

Description

Buckwheat is a cozy alternative to your breakfast oatmeal - with a chewy and heartier texture.

Ingredients

  • buckwheat - 1 cup
  • milk - 3 cups
  • sugar - to taste or use honey
  • cinnamon - 2 pinches, optional

Steps

  1. Parboil the buckwheat in boiling water, cover and let it sit until swollen nicely.
  2. Then mix this buckwheat porridge with the warm milk, in which you have dissolved the sugar / honey and a little cinnamon if desired.
